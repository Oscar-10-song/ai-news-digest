Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Frontier Daily?
AI Frontier Daily is a daily AI news digest newsletter. Every morning at 8 AM, it scans 6 sources — arXiv, HuggingFace, Hacker News, Reddit, GitHub Trending, and industry news — then uses DeepSeek AI to curate 5-8 must-read AI updates. One email. Zero noise.
How is this different from other AI newsletters?
Most newsletters either dump links or rely on human editors who can't cover everything. AI Frontier Daily uses AI as an editor that reads every paper, post, and repo across 6 sources, then picks only what truly matters. No algorithm bubble, no sponsored content.
